Index to the GrAL and related modules

GrAL is the Grid Algorithms Library.
GrAL consist of a growing number of grid-related modules, and is bundled with some low-level support modules, which are not part of GrAL in a proper sense, but are used at some places.

Look here for the top level entry point to the documentation of all (support and GrAL) modules.
